FAQ

How can I get a quote?

You have two different options, artist or client. It depends on your needs and skills. In case you just need to hire an artist, you should choose client. On the other hand, if you want to earn money showing up your amazing cleverness you have to create the account as an artist.


How much will it cost to commission an artist?

Artists charge according to how the event is going to be, rather than a specific hourly rate. Therefore, the fee to commission work for the time and the duration of the performance.


How do I pay for the work?

We take 50% of the full charge when the client hire the artist (when he confirm his order) and 50% at the end of the final artwork. You should pay by credit card or debit card.

What happen as a client If cancel at the last moment a performance?

It is going to be charge a fee for the performance that has been cancelled, it will be different, depending on the artist.



How long it will take?

The duration of performance depend on the customer needs. At least 1 hour and a half up to 4 hours. It should be based on the demand of the artist.



What does the artist need to know from me?

As a client you should fill the profile in order to know a little bit more about your interests. So we can suggest different type of artist based on your preferences.
